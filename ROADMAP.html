⬡ SWARM DEFENSE

Development Roadmap — Godot 4.7 — Last updated 2026-06-23
Game Loop: You pilot a spacecraft launching from Earth's atmosphere. A Swarm attacks immediately — text popups and objective guides walk you through establishing a forward operating base. Build solar panels for power, turrets for defense, extractors for resources. The camera follows your ship by default (your main character). Your ship has health, shields, ammo, and upgrade slots (hull, shields, weapons, engines). Research new tech to unlock stronger buildings and ship modules. Survive escalating wave defenses with up to 16 players in co-op.
